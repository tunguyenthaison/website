UNDERGRADUATE THESIS

    (May 31, 2015.)
    • Topic: "Linear control systems and the regularity of the minimum time functions."
    • Supervisor: Khai T. Nguyen
    • Abstract: Consider the linear control system x′(t)=Ax(t)+Bu(t)x′(t)=Ax(t)+Bu(t) with the target 00. The function T(x)T(x) is the minimum time needed to steer a point xx to 00. It is well-known that under the Rank-Kalman condition, TT is just continuous in the reachable set. Our main goal is to study the regularity of T(x)T(x). We prove that TT is a.e. twice differentiable. Moreover, we also obtain an explicit formula for the set of singularities where the optimal control changes its sign.
